Testing

Testing is a set of procedure intended to establish the Quality,Perfomance,Compatabilty,Functional,Behavioral and Relaibility.. of a product or service before comming into usage, It checks the Customer requirements in all aspects of view
If even all the Requirements are fullfilled by the Software. The hardware configuration such as OS or Device or Browser or App also be considered as a part of testing

Software Testing

Software testing is the process of executing a software application under test such as functional,behavioral.. with an intention of finding bugs and errors before the application is deployed into product environment

Verification and validation is done on Application under Test(AUT)

Why Testing is Important

The testing is important in various aspects to check the product or service met the business requirements they are mentioned below

  • Saves money by avoiding defects in early stages
  • All business requirements are implemented
  • Quality of the product or service
  • Relaibility of the product or service
  • Functionalities are behaving as its expected
  • Behavioral is same as the requirements
  • Application is secure enough against security threats
  • Doesn't break under working circumstances
  • Doesn't harm the business reputation
  • Compatability that is Works well on all supported OS devices and screens
  • Customer satisfication
