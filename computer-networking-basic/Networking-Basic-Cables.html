Network Cable

Network cable is the medium through which the information or data moves from one device to another. The cable has many types. According to the network topology, protocol and size the cable is choosen

Coaxial cable

Coaxial cable is a type of electric cable that has Inner conductor surrounded by a tubular insulating layer surrounded by a tubular conducting metallic shield and an outer cover called the sheath or jacket.In this type of transmission line high frequency of electric signals is carried with less losses.

Cable types

   The cables are mainly classified into two types based on the transmission.

Twisted pair

It is a type of cabling in which two conductors of a single circuit twisted together for the purpose of electromagnetic compatiblity. It reduces electromagnetic radiation it is classified into two types

  • Unshielded Twisted Pair(UTP)
  • Shielded Twisted Pair (STP)

    • Unshielded Twisted Pair(UTP)

    It is found in many ethernet networks and telephone systems. It is made with copper wire of 22 or 24 AWG (American wire Guage)

    Unshielded twisted pair

    Shielded Twisted Pair (STP)

    It is found in many ethernet networks and telephone systems. It is made with copper wire of 22 or 24 AWG (American wire Guage)
