Domain

The company or Industry have its own Business Model, Expectations and challenges, Each Employee of the Company Couldn't know all the aspects of business let we go through a life cycle of a Product through simple term

For Example : Mobile Phone

Hardware:

  • A mobile Phone Hardware is Produced by set of Employees Called Hardware Developer
  • It is Tested By a Hardware tester(Quality Engineer)
  • It has a Support engineer to help the Product in all the aspects of view
  • These are all managed by the manager in all Fields

    • Software :

  • Similarly the software Part also have a Developer, tester , support etc..

    • Customer care:

  • When the Product is launched it can't be serviced by set of employees like Guiding the usage of the Products in all aspect of view
  • other Names : Domain / Role / Designation
